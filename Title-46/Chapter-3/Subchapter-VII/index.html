§ 46–307.01. Proceeding to determine parentage.

§ 46–307.01. Proceeding to determine parentage.

A court of the District authorized to determine parentage of a child may serve as a responding tribunal in a proceeding to determine parentage brought under this chapter or a law or procedure substantially similar to this chapter.

History

(Feb. 9, 1996, D.C. Law 11-81, § 701, 42 DCR 6748; June 22, 2006, D.C. Law 16-137, § 2(g), 53 DCR 3634.)
