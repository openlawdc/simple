§ 26–551.22. Prohibition of fraud.

§ 26–551.22. Prohibition of fraud.

A financial institution shall not engage in a fraudulent activity or an act against the public interest.

History

(June 9, 2001, D.C. Law 13-308, § 122, 48 DCR 3244.)
