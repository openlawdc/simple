§ 47-2023. Rules and regulations.

The Mayor may issue rules and regulations to carry out the purposes of this chapter.

History

(May 27, 1949, 63 Stat. 121, ch. 146, title I, § 143; July 24, 1982, D.C. Law 4-131, § 211, 29 DCR 2418; enacted, Apr. 9, 1997, D.C. Law 11-254, § 2, 44 DCR 1575.)
