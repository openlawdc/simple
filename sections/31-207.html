§ 31–207. Required annual reports of Commissioner - Publication and distribution.

After May 18, 1910, the annual reports of the Commissioner of Insurance and Securities [Commissioner of the Department of Insurance, Securities, and Banking] shall be printed and bound in 1 volume, and shall be ready for distribution not later than the 1st day of the next regular session of Congress thereafter.

History

(May 18, 1910, 36 Stat. 379, ch. 248, § 1; May 21, 1997, D.C. Law 11-268, § 10(e), 44 DCR 1730.)
