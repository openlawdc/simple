§ 3–631. Establishment of Commission on Poverty. [Expired]

Expired.

History

(Sept. 19, 2006, D.C. Law 16-151, § 2, 53 DCR 5368.)

Legislative History of Law 16-151

Law 16-151, the "Commission on Poverty Establishment Act of 2006", was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 16-387 which was referred to the Committee on Human Services. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on May 2, 2006, and June 6, 2006, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on June 26, 2006, it was assigned Act No. 16-392 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 16-151 became effective on September 19, 2006.
