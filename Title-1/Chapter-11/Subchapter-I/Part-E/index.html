Legislative History of Law 3-58

Law 3-58 was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 3-158, which was referred to the Committee on Government Operations. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on January 22, 1980 and February 5, 1980, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on February 26, 1980, it was assigned Act No. 3-154 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review.
